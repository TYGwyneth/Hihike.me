Welcome.

#hihikeme is a service for hitchhikers who wants to hitch a ride or the ones who want to give someone a ride. By simply taking a picture with Instagram and the hashtag #hihikeme you can show others where they can pick you up, and where you are heading. If you have a ride and want some company, you can easily see who is close and needs a lift, and where they want to go.

  • Upload your picture on instagram, write when and where you want to go and use the hashtag #hihikeme.
  • Your picture will appear on the website, so that people can see where you are and where want to go.
  • Here is someone who is going to the same place. A perfect match!.
